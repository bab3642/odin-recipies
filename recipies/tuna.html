Tuna Sandwich

Description

This is my favorite recipe for a tuna sandwich. It combines two well known recipies to make a killer, tasty tuna sandwich that is not to dry and still has a nice crunch. My wife and I eat it at least once a week! Make sure you use good quality tuna to really enhance the flavor.

This recipe feeds my wife and I for 2 meals (4 servings total). Give it a try, I know you will love it!

Ingredients

  • Two 5oz cans of tuna (use the best quality you can afford)
  • Eight slices of your favorite loaf of bread for sandwiches
  • Celery (1/4 cup)
  • Pickle relish (1tbsp)
  • Butter (for toast)
  • lemon juice (1/2 tbsp)
  • One hard boiled egg
  • Mayonnaise (1/2 to 3/4 cup)
  • One half red onion
  • One clove of garlic
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • two large leaves of lettuce

Steps

  1. Drain the tuna and combine with minced garlic, sliced celery, diced onioin, chopped egg, relish, lemon juice and mayonnaise in a large mixing bowl. Mix thoroughly and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Toast and butter bread
  3. Wash and dry lettuce, folding it to fit on the sandwich
  4. Assemble sandwiches with a heaping scoop of tuna mixture and top it with the lettuce
  5. That's it! Enjoy!
