Tuscan Spaghetti

Description:

This is a recipe for Tuscan Spaghetti. This is a hearty dish with meat, peppers, and cheese. Come hungry because it will fill you up for a while

This recipe should make 6-8 servings. Enjoy the taste of Tuscany!

Ingredients

  • Parmesan cheese
  • Spaghetti
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Chicken Bouillion Paste
  • Ground Italian Sausage
  • Tuscan seasoning
  • Onion

Directions

  1. Boil water to cook spaghetti according to package directions. While spaghetti is cooking, chop veggies
  2. Saute ground pork until cooked thoroughly
  3. Saute vegetables until translucent
  4. Combine spaghetti, veggies, meat, crushed tomato, and tuscan seasoning
  5. add cheese, salt and pepper to taste
  6. Enjoy!
