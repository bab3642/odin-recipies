Spaghetti

Description

This is the recipe for Spaghetti. Not just any spaghetti, but the best damned Spaghetti your grandmother never made! You only need to buy three ingredients, it will be the easiest spaghetti you have ever made. The tomato sauce will tingle your tongue and the noodles will squelch under the soft bite from your palatte.

Give it a try, I know you will love it

Ingredients

  • Rao's Spaghetti Sauce
  • Spaghetti noodle or pasta of your choice (I prefer angel hair)
  • Trader Joe's frozen meatballs

Steps

  1. Turn the oven to 400 degrees Farenheit
  2. Once the oven is heated, put in the meatballs for 15-20min
  3. While the meatballs are cooking, boil a large pot of water, adding a dash of salt
  4. Once the water is boiling, stir in the spaghetti noodles and cook according to package
  5. Drain the noodles and combine all three ingredients (Noodles, sauce, and meatballs)
  6. Voila! Enjoy your spaghetti. Optional toppings are parmesan cheese and dried basil
